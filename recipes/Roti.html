MAKE DELICIOUS SRILANKAN ROTI

Wondering How Tamil People Make That Delicious Roti? Look No Further!

Tamil people love to eat roti! They don't make it the indian traditional way. Here you will see step by step procedure on how to make authentic Sri Lanakn Pol Roti packed with flavor!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Shredded Coconut
  • 1 Cup Chopped Onion
  • 1/4 Cup Green Chilies
  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 1 Tbsp Canola Oil

Steps

  1. Sift the All-Purpose Flour (3 cups) . Add Salt (to taste) , Coconuts (1 cup) and Canola Oil (1 Tbsp) to the bowl and mix well
  2. Add Water (1 cup) a little at a time until it forms a dough. The dough should be like play-dough consistency, not sticky.
  3. Add the Onion (1/4) and Chili Peppers (1/4 cup) to the dough.
  4. Shape the dough into balli, then flatten the dough ball with your hands into a thick disc.
  5. To cook the roti, heat a heavy-duty skillet or cast-iron skillet on medium-high heat. Once hot, cook on both sides until you get them well browned.
  6. Cook the rest of the roti.
  7. Serve and enjoy with a curry or meat dish.
